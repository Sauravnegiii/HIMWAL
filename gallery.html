Program Highlights

Targeted action for a resilient Himalayan future.

Bhojan Vikas Karyakram

Millet Nutrition Program

Educating rural women in the Joshimath block about the nutritional values and benefits of consuming traditional crops like millets and pulses. This initiative includes expert-led training sessions focusing on healthy recipes and sustainable food habits.

Beej Sanrakhshan Karyakram

Traditional Seeds Collection

We are collecting and preserving traditional seeds from various high-altitude villages in Uttarakhand to protect valuable genetic resources. These seeds are distributed to progressive farmers to ensure long-term agricultural sustainability.

Paryavaran Jagrukta Karyakram

Environment Awareness Program

Organizing environmental programs and online webinars to engage the youth. We actively celebrate Wildlife Week, World Wetlands Day, National Science Day, World Water Day, and Earth Day with activities ranging from expert lectures to student painting competitions.

Lok Sanskriti Sanrakshan

Cultural Heritage Conservation

Documenting the tangible and intangible assets of the Painkhanda (Joshimath) Development Block. We proudly collaborate with institutions like the Doon Library and Research Center to promote and protect traditional folk instruments like the Dhol.

Livelihood Support

Women's Empowerment

As part of our broader mission, we conduct skill development and sustainable livelihood generation programs to empower Himalayan women and strengthen local rural economies.

Community Outreach

Miscellaneous Initiatives

Beyond our core goals, HIMWAL Society actively participates in grassroots development, health initiatives, and community support programs spanning both rural areas and urban centers across Uttarakhand to foster holistic resilience.
