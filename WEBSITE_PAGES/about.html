About Us

Empowering women in the Himalayas through awareness, skills, and opportunities.

Who We Are

Himalayan Women Awareness & Livelihood Society (HIMWAL) is dedicated to uplifting women in Himalayan regions by providing access to education, vocational training, and livelihood opportunities. Our approach blends cultural preservation with modern development, ensuring that women can thrive without losing their heritage.

Our Mission

To empower women in Himalayan communities with knowledge, skills, and resources so they can lead independent and sustainable lives, preserving their cultural identity while gaining economic freedom.

Our Vision

A future where women in the Himalayas are equal contributors to their communities, resilient leaders, and custodians of both tradition and progress.

"When you empower a woman, you empower an entire community."

Our Values

Inclusivity

We design programs that reach every woman, regardless of caste, religion, or literacy level.

Sustainability

All our initiatives prioritise long-term impact over short-term gains, respecting both people and nature.

Community First

Programs are co-designed with local women's groups and village elders to ensure relevance and ownership.

Transparency

We maintain open reporting of our finances, activities, and outcomes to all our stakeholders.

Cultural Pride

We celebrate Himalayan heritage and weave cultural preservation into every livelihood programme.

Equity

Gender equity and social justice are the foundations upon which all our advocacy work is built.
