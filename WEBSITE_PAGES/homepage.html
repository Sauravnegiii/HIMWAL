About HIMWAL

HIMWAL serves remote Himalayan communities by facilitating awareness campaigns, skill development programs, and livelihood initiatives designed for women and girls. We combine cultural preservation with modern, sustainable approaches to empower communities.

Our Mission

To empower women in the Himalayan region through awareness, education, skill-building, and sustainable livelihoods—while preserving cultural identity and promoting gender equity at community and policy levels.

Our Vision

A Himalayan region where every woman has the knowledge, resources, and confidence to lead a sustainable livelihood and contribute to resilient communities.

Focus Areas

Education & Skills

Vocational training, adult literacy, and digital literacy courses tailored to local economies.

Health & Wellness

Community health camps, maternal health awareness, and mental wellbeing support for women and girls.

Advocacy & Rights

Legal literacy, women’s rights campaigns, and local policy engagement to secure equal opportunities.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Craft clusters, agro-based enterprises, and eco-tourism initiatives designed with women entrepreneurs.

Cultural Preservation

Traditional craft revival and intergenerational programs to protect local knowledge.

Climate Resilience

Training and local projects that strengthen resilience to environmental change and support sustainable practices.
