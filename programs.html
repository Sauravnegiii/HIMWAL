Our Core Initiatives

Focused programs addressing environmental conservation, livelihoods, and cultural preservation in Uttarakhand.

Home › Programs

Transforming Communities

Targeted grassroots action through four foundational programs.

Health & Nutrition

Bhojan Vikas Karyakram

A millet nutrition program educating rural women, particularly in the Joshimath block, about the nutritional values of the millets and pulses they cultivate. Through expert training sessions and recipe sharing, we aim to positively influence traditional food habits.

Agriculture

Beej Sanrakhshan Karyakram

An initiative focused on traditional seed collection and preservation. We gather native seeds from various high-altitude villages in Uttarakhand and distribute them to progressive farmers to ensure the sustainability of these vital agricultural resources.

Awareness

Paryavaran Jagrukta Karyakram

Organizing environmental awareness programs and online webinars with school and college students. We engage youth through expert lectures, painting competitions, quizzes, and by celebrating key events like National Wildlife Week, World Water Day, and Earth Day.

Heritage

Lok Sanskriti Sanrakshan

A cultural heritage conservation initiative focused on documenting and protecting traditions facing disappearance. Our Phase I work targets the diverse Painkhanda (Joshimath) block, organizing events that celebrate traditional arts, rituals, and iconic folk instruments like the Dhol.
