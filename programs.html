Our Programs

Focused initiatives creating lasting change in Himalayan communities

Education & Empowerment

Building knowledge and skills to transform lives across Uttarakhand

Education

Education & Skills

Vocational training, adult literacy, and digital literacy courses in Uttarakhand schools. We equip women with practical skills to enter the workforce and start small businesses from their homes.

Children

Children's Education

Supporting classroom learning in Dehradun and remote Himalayan villages. We work with local teachers, parents, and communities to ensure children receive quality foundational education.

Environment

Environment Awareness

World Day for Glaciers programs teaching children about glacier preservation and the importance of protecting the water sources that sustain Himalayan life and downstream communities.

Community

Community Outreach

Mission Life campaigns for biodiversity conservation across Uttarakhand. Our teams engage local communities in hands-on conservation activities tied to sustainable living practices.

Climate

Climate Resilience

Training youth on glacier preservation and climate action in Himalayan regions. We build the next generation of environmental stewards who will advocate for their communities.

Livelihoods

Livelihood Support

Seed grants and microfinance support for women-led businesses. We help women form self-help groups, access credit, and build sustainable income sources rooted in local tradition.
