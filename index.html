About HIMWAL

HIMWAL (Himalayan Women Awareness and Livelihood Society) is dedicated to empowering women across Uttarakhand's remote Himalayan communities.

We work in Chamoli, Dehradun, and surrounding villages to provide education, skill development, and sustainable livelihood opportunities. Our programs blend cultural preservation with modern approaches, ensuring women can thrive while maintaining their heritage.

Our Mission

To empower women through awareness, education, and sustainable livelihoods while preserving cultural identity.

Our Vision

A Himalayan region where every woman leads a sustainable livelihood and contributes to resilient communities.
